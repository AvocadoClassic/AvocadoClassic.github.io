Non-Profit DAO

Avocado Classic Inc. is a not-for-profit Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) that utilizes data mining and blockchain technology to generate funds for renewable energy technology and disperse it's use to improvished areas worldwide.

Novel Renewable energy projects.

Refundable Smart Contracts*

All contracts are refundable before activation. Upon activation ethereum will be sold to fund the project and 100 tokens will be dispersed. Those tokens can then be sold on a decentralized exchange or hed to collect a dividend.
